About Us

Kishorepur Chatrasangha proudly organizes Maha Shivaratri, celebrating Lord Shiva with devotion, culture, and joy. Our event features spiritual rituals, cultural performances, and grand stage shows, bringing the community together in faith and festivity. Join us in this divine celebration! Har Har Mahadev!
